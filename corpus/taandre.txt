T.A. Andre  is a general contracting firm that specializes in industrial,
commercial, institutional and municipal construction projects in Eastern
Ontario, Canada.  
  
T.A. Andres  clients have come to depend on the integrity & stability of the
management team and the staff to help them complete new construction,
renovations, upgrades and repairs.

construction requirements. Furthermore, we have the necessary equipment to
expedite construction projects.
