  CELEBRATING SINCE 1980

Two generations of innovative leaders have grown InterAll Limited to be an
all-encompassing industry leader. InterAll Ltd. offers a full spectrum of
renovation and contracting services to a diverse client base in the Greater
Toronto Area. From planning & design, through all phases of construction,
InterAll Ltd. provides the expertise and trades required to make your project
a successful one. Our commitment to customer satisfaction, quality
craftsmanship, design excellence and sustainable renovation practices are the
building blocks of our company.

In 1980, InterAll Limited was founded by Giovanni Farro. Giovannis successor,
Johnny Farro, after 20 years of growing up in the family business is proud to
evolve the vision of the organization and continue to contribute to the
community.
